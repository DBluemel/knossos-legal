FAQ – Knossos

Last updated: July 2026

What is Knossos?

Knossos is a circular maze puzzle game. Each level presents a unique labyrinth built from concentric rings. Your goal is to navigate from the outer edge to the green dot at the center in as few steps as possible.

How do I play?

When a level starts, choose one of the glowing entry points on the outside of the maze. Tap it to enter, and the door closes behind you. From there, tap any neighboring cell to move into it — you can move inward, outward, clockwise, or counter-clockwise, as long as there is no wall between you and the target cell. Reach the center to complete the level.

How are stars awarded?

Each level has an optimal (shortest possible) solution. Your star rating depends on how close you get to that optimal step count:

  • 3 stars — you matched or beat the optimal route
  • 2 stars — within 10% of the optimal step count
  • 1 star — within 25% of the optimal step count
  • 0 stars — more than 25% above optimal, or you used the hint

What does the hint button do?

The hint button (lightbulb icon) automatically guides you to the center via the shortest available route from your current position. Using the hint completes the level but awards 0 stars. Use it if you are truly stuck — there is no penalty beyond the star rating.

What does the restart button do?

The restart button (circular arrow icon) resets the current level from scratch. Your step count returns to zero and you choose a new entry point. Your previously saved best result for that level is not affected.

How do I unlock new levels?

Levels unlock one by one as you complete them. Finish a level — regardless of your star rating — to unlock the next one.

How do I remove ads?

You can purchase the "Remove Ads" option from the Settings screen. This is a one-time payment that permanently removes all advertisements from the app. The purchase is processed through Google Play and is linked to your Google account, so it can be restored if you reinstall the app.

Is my progress saved?

Yes. Your level results (best step count and star rating) and your current in-progress level are saved automatically on your device. Progress is stored locally and is not backed up to any server. If you uninstall the app or clear its storage, your progress will be lost.

Does the app collect my personal data?

Knossos itself does not collect or transmit your personal data. The free version displays ads through Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers and other information for advertising purposes. Please see our Privacy Policy for details.
