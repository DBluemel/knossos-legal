Privacy Policy for Knossos

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Knossos ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application. We built Knossos to be a simple, offline-friendly puzzle game, and we keep data collection to a minimum.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Knossos does not require account creation or sign-in. We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, or any other personal information directly. Your game progress (level results, step counts, stars, and settings) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to us.

2. Legal Basis for Processing (EEA/UK Users)

Where required by applicable law, advertising-related data processing is based on your consent obtained through Google's consent mechanisms or other lawful bases provided by Google and its partners.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Knossos displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process certain data, including device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID), IP address, and general usage data and other information required for advertising functionality., in order to serve ads and measure ad performance. This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's own privacy policy.

You can learn more about how Google uses this data here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Depending on your device and region, you may be able to opt out of personalized advertising through your device's ad settings.

4. In-App Purchases

Knossos offers an optional one-time purchase to remove advertisements. This purchase is processed entirely through Google Play's billing system. We do not receive or store your payment information; this is handled directly by Google in accordance with the Google Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy

Knossos is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If the App is used by children, advertisements are served through AdMob in accordance with Google's applicable policies and settings.

6. Data Security

We do not maintain servers or databases containing user gameplay data. Since gameplay data is stored locally on your device, you can remove all locally stored data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing its storage through your device settings.

Users in certain regions may be presented with a consent message allowing them to choose whether personalized advertisements may be shown. Consent choices may be managed through the consent options provided by Google, within the App where available, or through device settings, depending on your region and device.

7. International Data Transfers

Data processed by Google may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. Such processing is governed by Google's privacy practices.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Third-Party Services

Knossos uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases)

These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.
